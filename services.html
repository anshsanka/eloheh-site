Our Services

“Promoting Well-being for Every Community”

ElohehCare Wellness Kits

What: Personalized monthly boxes of supplements, teas, aromatherapy, and self-care items.

Why: To boost immunity, manage stress, and support restful sleep.

Eloheh Virtual Clinic

What: Secure telehealth visits with doctors, therapists, and dietitians.

Why: Deliver on-demand care—from prescriptions to mental health—anywhere.

Eloheh Home Diagnostics

What: Mail-in kits for glucose, food sensitivities, hormone & vitamin panels.

Why: Empower private, proactive health monitoring without clinic visits.

Eloheh Health Tracker App

What: Free app that syncs with wearables to log symptoms, meds, and test results.

Why: Give users one secure hub for all their health data and reminders.

Eloheh Community Wellness

What: Workshops, webinars, and fitness classes focused on preventive health.

Why: Build knowledge & support networks in underserved neighborhoods.

How to Get Started

  1. Select a service above.
  2. Book online or call our office.
  3. Complete your intake forms.
  4. Receive your service—in person, virtually, or by mail.

Key Benefits

  • Focus on prevention & education
